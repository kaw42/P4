Asian Cuisine in the United States

Origin of Dishes

Chicken Fried Rice

Fried rice first developed during the Sui Dynasty in China. To this day, all fried rice dishes can trace their origins to Chinese fried rice. Many varieties of fried rice have their own specific list of ingredients.

Nian Gao

Niangao has a history of at least 1,000 years. Early in the Liao Dynasty, people in Beijing had the custom of eating New Year cakes on the first day of the first month of the lunar year.During the Ming Dynasty and the Qing Dynasty , Niangao had already become a common folk snack, and remains so today.

Wonton Noodle Soup

Wontons originate from China, with evidence of its existence going as far back as the Qing Dynasty. Wonton soup was enjoyed by the rich, upper class families but after World War II, it became more widespread.
