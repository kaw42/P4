Asian Cuisine in the United States

How do they taste?

Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken fried rice has a combination of endless flavors! With hints of umami and sesame, the chicken adds a meaty flavor. Ingredients such as spring onion and carrots add a splash of freshness and the vibrant flavors combined with the warmness of freshly cooked fried rice gives the consumer a homey feel.

Nian Gao

Niangao has a sticky texture similar to that of rice cake or mochi. The subtle sweetness and hints of nuttyness brings out an immaculate flavor. Those who take one bite of this traditional dessert will surely go back in for a second, third, and fourth!

Wonton Noodle Soup

Wontons noodle soup brings back the memory of home to many chinese children. Previously a delicacy in the Qing Dynasty, this noodle soup dish can now be found in affordable chinese restaurants and is a classic sick day dish. The combination of the meaty wontons with the light soup brings out notes of a rich chicken broth base. The vegetables in the soup bring out a freshness as well!
